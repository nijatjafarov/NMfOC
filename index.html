Numerical Methods for Optimal Control

Numerical methods for optimal control involve algorithms and computational techniques to determine the best possible control strategy for a dynamic system, subject to constraints. These methods are essential in fields such as engineering, robotics, economics, and aerospace, where systems are governed by differential equations and optimization is required. In this website, programs of some of these methods are built for users.
